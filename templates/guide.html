About

Hi there, this is a small site which acts as a guide to the heroes of the Hero Association from One Punch Man. The following is a guide on how you can use this site to populate, correct or delete information from the database for this site:

How do I add a hero?

You can add a hero to the site by following these steps:

  1. Click on the 'Add New Hero' button in the navigation bar.
  2. Fill in the fields - if information for a particular field is unknown, please mark it as 'n/a'.
  3. Click on 'Add hero'
And your hero is added!

How do I edit a hero's info?

You can edit a hero on the site by following these steps:

  1. Click on the 'Edit Hero' button in the particular hero's card.
  2. Fill in the fields/change information. If information for a particular field is unknown, please mark it as 'n/a'.
  3. Click on 'Save changes'
And your hero has been updated!

How do I delete a hero?

You can delete a hero on the site by following these steps:

  1. Click on the 'Edit Hero' button in the particular hero's card.
  2. Fill in the fields/change information. If information for a particular field is unknown, please mark it as 'n/a'.
  3. Click on 'Delete'
And your hero has been deleted!
